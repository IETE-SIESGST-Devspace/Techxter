About Techxter

TECHXTER 10.0 is a National Level Technical Paper Presentation, open to all the branches of engineering. This event is specially designed in such a way that it tests all aspects of the students' knowledge on varied topics. It provides the participants an excellent opportunity for demonstrating technical competence and presentation skills and innovative ideas with Emerging Technologies. Students can use this event as a platform to showcase different and upcoming technologies. TECHXTER 10.0 would be held on April 17, 2021. We are thrilled to celebrate the anniversary of Techxter with its 10th Iteration.Techxter has embraced the Theme "Emerging Technologies", to emphasize and understand the importance of technology, and to highlight the influence of Technical advancements in different fields.

Who We Are

The South Indian Education Society (SIES) was pioneered in 1932. What started with a strength of six students has now become a conglomerate of various institutions with over 18,000 students under its wing. SIES has established a high school, an array of arts, science and commerce colleges, along with academic and professional institutions of higher learning. The single minded objective to impart Knowledge that meets the increasing need of common man at all levels has driven SIES through the past 80 years. South Indian Education Society has constantly endeavoured to give back to society by making significant contributions to the upliftment of society through its mutli-prolonged initiatives in cultural, social and educational fields.

The IETE Students' Forum of SIES GST, Nerul, was inaugurated in March 2008. This Student Chapter plays an active role in organizing extra-curricular events. ISF believes in originality and has strived to always incorporate new and innovative ideas in all projects and activities. Our motto is to encourage teamwork and the spark to wander into newer and unexplored territories in our students. Major events held under the banner of ISF are Techxter- A national-level technical paper presentation competition; Ideation- An idea presentation competition; Bootcamps & Seminars- on upcoming technologies. ISF organizes workshops on trending technologies to expose students to concepts and tools apart from the prescribed syllabus.

Rules

Guidelines

  1. Submitted paper will be checked for plagiarism
  2. Submitted Papers must include the Name, Affiliation, E-mail ID, and Mobile number of the first author just below the title on the first page.
  3. Names of all other participants must also be included.
  4. Participants must also mention the subject area upon which the subject topic is based.
  5. Participants must submit the full-length paper in a maximum of four A4 size pages in IEEE paper format.
  1. Techxter is open for all streams of engineering & polytechnic students.
  2. Paper should not contain plagiarized content.
  1. To submit a paper you must fill the google form and upload your paper.
  2. Participants are allowed to submit multiple papers, after shortlisting and acceptance of papers any one paper is allowed to be presented during the competition.
  3. After the paper submission, the papers get reviewed by our Faculty.
  4. Participants whose papers are selected will receive an email about the confirmation and a call from our Outreach Team.
  5. The event will be conducted online.
