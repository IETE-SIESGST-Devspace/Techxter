Rules

Guidelines

  1. To submit a paper you must fill the google form and upload your paper.
  2. Participants are allowed to submit multiple papers, after shortlisting and acceptance of papers any one paper is allowed to be presented during the competition.
  3. After the paper submission, the papers get reviewed by our Faculty.
  4. Participants whose papers are selected will receive an email about the confirmation and a call from our Outreach Team.
  5. The event will be conducted online.

Testimonial

I congratulate the IETE Students' forum of SIES Graduate School of technology on their success in organising 'TechXter', a national level technical paper presentation.The event has gone a long way in providing an interactive platform for students all over to showcase the state of art in information and communication technologies and to exchange knowledge that encompasses a broad range of disciplines. It is hoped that the participants have benefitted from the exposure and gathered ideas for facilitating research. This event has provided an excellent opportunity to confidently present ideas and hone presentation skills." "I appreciate the zeal, hard work and enthusiasm with which the IETE students' forum of SIES GST has brought this event to fruition. The event was a grand success and hope it brings an invigorating change to young researchers.
